Lab 8: A/DCs & DACs (Lab sheet) DAC0808.pdfADC0801.pdf (includes 0804)

Use a DC offset on procedure 5 to recover the entire sine wave. You may want to use a 4Vpp sine wave or smaller so you can get a DC offset large enough to make a difference, and calibrate your A/DC for such.

In doing your spreadsheet, you will notice that the DAC while using the switches as your binary input will output a 0000 0000 as close to 0V and 1111 1111 as one bit increment below 10V. We would need the 9th bit to produce 10V.
